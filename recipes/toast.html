Toast

Descripton

Warm bread. Can be burnt, better if it's not.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Jam, optional

Steps

  1. Cut bread into desirable slices
  2. Using a toaster oven, toast the bread to the desired toastiness.
  3. Once done, spread butter on your toast.
  4. Add jam, if desired. Enjoy!
