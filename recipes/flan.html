Flan

Description

Flan is a milk based desert typically found in spanish cuisine. While a seemingly daunting dish to make, it's actually pretty easy to do!

Ingredients

  • white sugar, 1 cup
  • water, 1/2 cup
  • vanilla, 1 tsp
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can of full fat coconut milk
  • 4 eggs
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, salt, vanilla, sweetened condensed milk, and coconut milk.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the sugar and water, until golden brown.
  4. Once the sugar has begun to caramelize, and is a nice golden brown color, pour the sugar into a pie pan.
  5. Coat the pie pans base and sides with the melted sugar.
  6. Place the pie pan in a larger dish
  7. Fill the larger dish with hot or boiling water to cover halfway up the pie pan
  8. Bake the flan, covered, for 1 hour
  9. Once done, let the flan cool, then chill in the fridge for several hours.
