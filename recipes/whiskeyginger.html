Whisey Ginger

Description

Alcoholic beverage with crisp gingery taste.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of whiskey
  • Your choice of ginger ale or ginger beer

Steps

  1. Add some ice to a glass
  2. Pour 2 fingers of whiskey into the glass
  3. Add equal or more amounts of ginger ale/beer
  4. Garnish with lime wedge if you want
